How It Actually Works.

Curious about the technical details? Here is exactly how students sync their contacts, how texts are sent, and how your program securely receives the money.

📱

1. Syncing Student Contacts

The magic of Rallyfund is that students don't need to manually type in phone numbers or go back and forth with donors. Everything is handled through a seamless, automated flow.

    1

    Contact Syncing

    When a student joins your campaign, they download the Rallyfund app. The app securely scans their phone contacts (with permission) and identifies potential donors.

  • B

    Instant Syncing

    Upon approval, the app reads their address book and filters out irrelevant numbers. The student can select who to include, and the contacts are securely uploaded to our database within seconds.

  • C

    Privacy First

    Contact data is heavily encrypted. We never sell, share, or repurpose a student's contact list. It is strictly used for this single fundraising campaign.

2. AI-Optimized Message Sending

Traditional fundraisers use a generic "bot" number to blast out spam texts. We do something much more powerful and personal.

  • A

    AI Determines the Time

    Our backend AI analyzes the contact's timezone and demographic likelihood to determine the absolute perfect time they are likely to read a text and donate (e.g., Thursday at 6:30 PM).

  • B

    Push Notification to Student

    At that exact moment, we send a Push Notification to the student's phone: "Time to message Grandma Pat!"

  • C

    Sent from the Real Phone Number

    The student taps the notification, which opens their native iMessage/SMS app. The message (written by our AI) and the donor link are already pre-filled. The student just taps "Send". The message comes from the student's real phone number, ensuring a 99% open rate and destroying the "spam" filter.

3. Payments & How You Get the Money

We handle all PCI-compliant credit card routing through our integration with Stripe, the industry standard for online payments.

  • A

    Program Onboarding

    When a coach creates a campaign, they complete a secure "Stripe Connect" onboarding flow. You attach your school or booster club's official bank account.

  • B

    Donors Check Out

    Donors click the link in the student's text message and complete their checkout using Credit Card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.

  • C

    The Automated Split

    The second a donor pays $100, the Stripe API automatically splits the funds. Our 10% platform fee ($10) goes to Rallyfund, and the remaining 90% ($90) goes directly into your program's connected Stripe account. You can initiate a bank payout at any time.
